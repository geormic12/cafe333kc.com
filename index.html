Our Story

Built for the Music.
Built for Kansas City.

Kansas City raised this music. Café 333 is giving it a permanent seat at the table.

This city built jazz. The legends started here — and the talent never left. But somewhere along the way, the rooms did. Café 333 is the fix: a real jazz joint at 333 Southwest Blvd where local musicians play to local crowds, and nobody has to leave town to be heard.

Brought to you by the team behind Social Cabaret, we're combining a working stage with a serious kitchen — the kind of place where you come for the music and stay for the food, or the other way around. Either way, you're not leaving early.

Brass rails, brick walls, and a sound system that treats the music right. This is a room built for the artists who make this city what it is.

The Experience

Dinner, Drinks &
a Live Set

Not background music. Not a concert with a cover charge. Something in between — a night that moves at the pace of a great meal and a great band.

I

The Table

Seasonal small plates, craft cocktails, and a wine list that holds its own. Our kitchen doesn't play second fiddle — the food is as much a reason to come as the music. Pull up a seat and let the evening start right.

II

The Stage

This is a working stage for working musicians. Kansas City's own — vocalists, pianists, bass players, horn sections — performing original work and standards that deserve to be heard in a room this close. You feel every note.

III

The Room

The best part of a night out is the part you didn't plan — the conversation that lingers, the musician who joins your table, the stranger who knows the words. Café 333 is built for those moments. No velvet ropes. Just people and music.
