Our Story

A Place Where Art
Finds Its Home

Café 333 is born from a simple belief: that an evening out should feed your soul as much as your appetite.

Brought to you by the creative minds behind Social Cabaret, Kansas City's beloved dinner theater company, Café 333 blends live jazz, intimate theatrical performances, and chef-driven cuisine into something entirely its own.

We're building a space at 333 Southwest Blvd where musicians, actors, chefs, and you — our audience — come together in one room, sharing one experience. No velvet ropes. No pretension. Just the real, electric warmth of people gathered around great art and great food.

This is a jazz club with a theater soul and a kitchen that takes the stage just as seriously as the performers do.

The Experience

Three Acts,
One Unforgettable Evening

Every night at Café 333 is composed like a great performance — with movement, rhythm, and surprise.

I

The Table

Begin with flavors that set the mood — seasonal small plates, craft cocktails, and a wine list curated to complement both the food and the evening's performance. Our kitchen doesn't just serve dinner; it opens the show.

II

The Stage

From smoky jazz standards to original compositions, from one-act plays to spoken word — our stage is a living thing. Performers and audience share the same air, the same moment. This isn't background music. This is the reason you came.

III

The Connection

The best part of any night out is what happens in between — the conversation that lingers, the stranger who becomes a friend, the artist who joins your table after the set. Café 333 is designed for these moments.
